Goal:

To be healthy in body and mind, and to live a fuller and longer life.

At Fitness Center Carrillo we believe in wellness. Health is our priority, as it represents the most important aspect on anyone’s life. That is why we have optimised our facilities and services to the maximum to achieve the triangle of health: Physical, mental, and social.

Since our gym is located on the beach, you can improve your health while treating your mental well-being by overlooking the best views of Carrillo beach. Our 13,000 square meter facilities provide experienced personal trainers and the latest fitness equipment in the industry to guarantee health improvement.
